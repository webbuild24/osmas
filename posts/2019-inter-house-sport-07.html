THE 19TH ANNUAL INTER-HOUSE SPORT COMPETITION

Written by Mrs. O. O. Masominu ,Nov 6, 2019

We humbly appreciate our esteemed guests (all protocols duelly observed)

A high functioning team can achieve incredible results and work becomes motivating,fun and incredibly rewarding..
Each house is a team with leaders to bring everyone together with a common purpose-"to win"

The principal encouraged support for each other by cheering winners and clapping for those who can't make it to the Finnish line

I plead with officials to perform thier duties without fear and favour so the competition won't be spoilt by poor officiating
Special thanks to our patrons and PTA,we thank you for your past and present support and we look forward to more assistance and pray that the lord Will replenish your purse in Jesus name.

Once again,my lord Bishop and school proprietor, the chairman and chairperson, BOG, PTA,invited schools,sport officials and guest,other protocols duly extended,I welcome you all to this 19th Annual inter house sports fiesta.Relax and God bless you as you enjoy the days activities
