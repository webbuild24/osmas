THE PTA AND ITS CONTRIBUTIONS TO THE SCHOOL

Written by OSMAS , Fri, Sept 2, 2018

  1. A 60KVA Pekins generating set was purchased for the school in February 2005 and was commissioned by the lord Bishop and Proprietor, Rt. Rev. Dr. M. O. Owadayo.
  2. In September 2005 the PTA purchased a 14-seater Toyota Hiace bus for the use of the school to mark its 10th
  3. 10 computer sets and two printers were purchase for the school in February 2006.
  4. 5KVA new Mikano generating set was bought for the school in November, 2006. It was installed and commissioned by the lord Bishop and Proprietor, Rt. Rev. Dr. M. O. Owadayo.
  5. A 4,000 litre water tank was installed to serve the kitchen and boys’ hostel in 2007.
  6. 40 ceiling fans (Binatone) was also donated to the School for installation into the Kitchen and Girls Hostel in October, 2007.
  7. A 4.5 KVA Tek generator was donated for use in the Administrative block in March 2008.
  8. 2 small refrigerators were purchased for the 2 main staff rooms in April 2008.
  9. Sporting equipments (spike shoes, 2 Olympic sized high jump foams etc) were donated for School sports in 2008.
  10. A new Sharp Digital Photocopier machine was purchased for the Examination Committee in April 2008.
  11. Soccer boots with shin guards and socks etc. were purchased for the school football team in April 2009.
  12. A two-day Transformational Teaching Development workshop was organized for the staff in joint partnership with the School Management by Van-Hud Management consult – “Every child has a gold in him that could be explored as teachers need to think beyond the circle” between 21st and 22nd April 2009.
  13. To boost water supply in the school, two wells with pumping machines were dug for the school in August, 2009.
  14. 25 White Marker Boards were installed into the classrooms and laboratories in August 2010.
  15. A 30 seater air conditional Toyota Bus in joint partnership with the School Management was purchased for the School in May, 2011.
  16. The PTA inaugurated the students’ Welfare Fund Scheme for students, who lose their parent/sponsor in May, 2012. Many students had since benefitted till SS 3 and are still benefitting from this scheme.
  17. Ten more computer sets were purchased for the school Computer Laboratory in September, 2013.
  18. Staff Training workshop in partnership with the School Management was organized for the School and other sister schools in 2013.
  19. Purchase of a Konica Minolta Universal Photocopier and Scanner in April, 2013.
  20. In the year 2014, a number of projects were carried out in the School. These include:
  • A 5.5 KVA Tek generator to service the laboratories and music studio.
  • Construction of a large viewing centre with benches in the boys’ hostel.
  • 3 flat-screened Television plus D.S.T.V. and monthly subscription for the boys’ hostel, Junior and Senior
  • Girls’ hostel.
  • Purchase of Transistor radios to staff rooms, Accounts and Administrative Offices.

The PTA also make it a point of duty to give gifts to all staff every Christmas, Payment for some part-time teachers

to augument some subjects.

Trainings are also organized for parents during PTA general meetings like Transformational parenthood by Van/Hud Management Consult in May, 2009.

Presently plans are in the pipeline to construct a befitting Examination Hall for the School by the parents.

Surely we can say like the Psalmist that:  The lines have fallen for us in pleasant places, yea we have a good heritage

Psalm 16:6.

The Lord will surely bless our parents to reap the fruits of their labours on these children in Jesus name.
